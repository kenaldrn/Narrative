Help & FAQ

How to Add Books?

Go to the Add Book page and fill in the book title, author, rating, and optionally a cover image URL and review. Then click "Add Book". Your book will be saved locally in your browser.

Where are my books saved?

Books you add are saved in your browser's localStorage. This means they are saved only on your device and will not sync to other devices or browsers.

How can I browse my books?

Visit the Browse Books page to see all your saved books. You can search by title or author.

Can I edit or delete books?

Currently, Narrativ only supports adding and viewing books. Editing or deleting is not yet implemented but is planned for future updates.

Need more help?

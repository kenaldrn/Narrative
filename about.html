About Narrativ

Narrativ is your personal book diary inspired by Letterboxd, designed to help you track, review, and organize your reading journey. Whether you are an avid reader or just starting, Narrativ makes it simple to keep a record of your favorite books, share your thoughts, and discover new reads.

This project is built with simplicity, accessibility, and community in mind. Our goal is to create a free and open platform where book lovers can feel at home.

Stay tuned for more features, and happy reading!
